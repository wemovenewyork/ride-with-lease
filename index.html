GOT QUESTIONS?

We've got answers.

What's the minimum booking time? ▼
Most vehicles require a 3-hour minimum. The Goliath requires a 4-hour minimum on weekends. Holiday and peak-season bookings may have extended minimums.
Is BYOB allowed? ▼
Yes — passengers 21+ are welcome to bring their own beverages. We ask that you drink responsibly and clean up after yourselves. Cleaning fees apply for excessive messes.
How far in advance should I book? ▼
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for regular dates. For holidays, New Year's Eve, prom season, and summer weekends, book 1–3 months ahead to guarantee availability.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
Cancellations made 72+ hours before your booking receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours forfeit the deposit. No-shows are non-refundable. Contact us for special circumstances.
Is a deposit required? ▼
Yes — a 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of your event before the ride begins. We accept Visa, MC, Amex, Cash, and Zelle.
Is gratuity included in the price? ▼
Gratuity is not included in the base rate. A 15–20% tip for your driver is customary and greatly appreciated. Our drivers go above and beyond to make your experience exceptional.
Do you charge for tolls and parking? ▼
Tolls and parking fees are passed through at cost and added to your final invoice. NYC congestion pricing may apply for trips south of 60th Street in Manhattan. We'll notify you in advance.
Can we make multiple stops? ▼
Absolutely. Multi-stop routes are our specialty — bar crawls, club hopping, venue transfers, you name it. Just provide your itinerary when booking and we'll handle the logistics.
